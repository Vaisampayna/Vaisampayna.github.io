About Me

I'm a Master's student at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur.

My research focuses on Secure Multi-Party Computation (MPC) and its applications in privacy-preserving technologies. I build cryptographic protocols that allow multiple parties to jointly compute — without revealing anything beyond the result.

I'm passionate about bridging theoretical cryptography and practical systems — from OLE protocols over prime fields to end-to-end encrypted mobile apps.
